Bacon shrimp recipe

Shrimp wrapped with bacon.

Ingredients

  • 16 jumbo Shrimp, fresh, raw, jumbo (11-15)
  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 8 strips thin sliced bacon, cut in half
  • 16 toothpicks
  • 2 tablespoons hot honey

Steps

  1. Place shrimp on a plate and sprinkle both sides with Cajun seasoning. Wrap each shrimp with 1 narrow strip of bacon and fasten with a toothpick. Refrigerate for 25 to 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  3. Mix together ricotta, 2 cups mozzarella cheese, and eggs.
  4. Air-fry shrimp for 3 minutes. Flip and continue cooking until bacon is lightly crispy, 4 to 5 minutes more. You may have to do 2 batches depending on the size of your air fryer.
  5. Remove shrimp to a plate and drizzle with hot honey. Serve immediately, being watchful of any toothpicks.
